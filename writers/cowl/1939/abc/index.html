To Louis Basky

worker, militant unionist, Marxist, proletarian revolutionist, party builder, tireless soldier in the war for the emancipation of the oppressed, who died August 18, 1938 at the age of fifty six, after forty one years of continuous service and sacrifice in the cause of his class, worn out by the strain of innumerable strikes, demonstrations, political strife and the daily grind of building the revolutionary movement.

Our comrade, Louis, is dead but the spirit, the faith and wisdom which are his legacy to us live on in the great truths of Marxism embodied in the following pages which inspire our movement. With the help of this pamphlet to which Louis contributed much in various ways other workers will come forward to close and swell the ranks of the army of the proletarian revolution.
