James Burnham & Max Shachtman

Intellectuals in Retreat

(January 1939)

From The New International, Vol. V No. 1, January 1939, pp. 3–22.
Transcribed & marked up by Einde O’ Callaghan for the Encyclopaedia of Trotskyism On-Line (ETOL).

A political analysis of the drift of the anti-Stalinist intellectuals from Marxism towards reformism – A critique of Sidney Hook, Max Eastman, Eugene Lyons, Benjamin Stolberg, Charles Yale Harrison and other critics of Bolshevism, where they stand and where they are going.
