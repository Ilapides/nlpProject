Keith Narey

“How Militant Helps Me Fight Cuts”

(12 November 1976)

From Militant, No. 330, 12 November 1976, p. 2.
Transcribed by Iain Dalton.
Marked up by Einde O’Callaghan for the Encyclopaedia of Trotskyism On-Line (ETOL).


Militant has highlighted the struggles of different public sector workers and explained the alternative to cuts by posing a planned economy and an increase in the public works programme. After reading the paper regularly members of Bradford Trades Council successfully moved the formation of a Public Services Sub-Committee to fight cuts.

 

Keith Narey
(Bradford NUPE)
