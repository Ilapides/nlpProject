Labour Student Campaign

(December 1977)

From Militant, No. 385, 9 December 1977.
Transcribed by Iain Dalton.
Marked up by Einde O’Callaghan for the Encyclopaedia of Trotskyism On-Line (ETOL).


The Labour Club at Bradford College, formed only last year, has got off to a good start this year, with several students signing up at a hurriedly organised freshers’ exhibition, including the President, Vice-President and an executive member of the students’ union.

Over 200 of the Labour Students’ Bulletin were distributed, and a leafleting campaign around the halls of residence, followed by a canvass for members is taking place this week. A successful freshers’ meeting was addressed by Gary Armitage, National President of the textile unions. Further meetings with the apprentices of the college are planned.
