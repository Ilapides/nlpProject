Sam Ryan

Internet Archive

Sam Ryan was co-initiater of the Vern-Ryan Tendency in the US Socialist Workers Party based in Los Angeles in the 1950s, with Denis Vern. The main purpose of this archive is to provide an index of works by Sam Ryan, Denis Vern and the Vern-Ryan Tendency included in various parts of the MIA and ETOL. We would like to especially thank the Revolutonary Regroupment web site for bringing these documents to our attention.
